ABOUT US

We are a religious organization registered under the registration number S.A 15857 in the United Republic of Tanzania. Our purpose is to preach the true holistic gospel of Jesus Christ and Proclaim God's Love and Salvation in Tanzania and the world according to the great commission (Mathew 28: 19 -20), and take part in various development projects and by cooperating with stakeholders in the spiritual and religious and social development sectors.

As part of the body of Christ in Tanzania, we invite volunteers in various careers and with different sets of skills to help in our faith-based activities, These are such as building churches and evangelism outreach, Education, Agriculture, Healthcare or Medical missions, Supporting Widows, orphans, livelihood etc

About International Calvary Fellowship

We are a religious organization registered under the registration number S.A 15857 in the United Republic of Tanzania. Our purpose is to preach the true holistic gospel of Jesus Christ and Proclaim God's Love and Salvation in Tanzania and the world according to the great commission (Mathew 28: 19 -20), and take part in various development projects and by cooperating with stakeholders in the spiritual and religious and social development sectors.

As part of the body of Christ in Tanzania, we invite volunteers in various careers and with different sets of skills to help in our faith-based activities, These are such as building churches and evangelism outreach, Education, Agriculture, Healthcare or Medical missions, Supporting Widows, orphans, livelihood etc

    Projects

    Making a difference

    Church Construction Initiative

    Building Sacred Spaces for Worship and Community Growth

    The Church Construction Initiative is driven by our commitment to foster a worship environment that is both comfortable and inspiring. This project involves building new churches to create conducive spaces for worship and spiritual growth on our campuses. The goal is to unite people in a community of faith, providing an atmosphere where they can deepen their relationship with God and build meaningful connections with one another. Through these construction projects, we aspire to strengthen the foundation of our community and enhance the overall spiritual experience for all attendees.

    Music Academy for Faith and Skill Development

    Harmonizing Faith and Talent Through Musical Education

    The Music Academy project offers a distinctive platform for individuals of all ages to learn music while fostering spiritual growth. Aimed at providing positive outlets for both the young and old, our academy focuses on engaging participants in wholesome activities. Beyond teaching musical skills, our program emphasizes essential values such as teamwork, discipline, and perseverance. By combining the joy of music with character-building elements, we aspire to create a holistic environment where individuals can not only develop their musical talents but also strengthen their faith and personal attributes.

    Empowering Lives through Self-Help Groups

    Fostering Support, Faith, and Financial Assistance

    Our organization is dedicated to supporting the less fortunate and widows within our community through the establishment of self-help groups. These groups serve as a holistic platform, offering both spiritual and financial assistance to those in need. The overarching goal is to create a secure and supportive environment where individuals can unite, sharing their experiences, receiving encouragement, and supporting one another in their faith journey. Through this initiative, we aim to empower lives by providing the necessary resources and a community-driven approach to uplift those facing challenges within our community.

    Nurturing Future Leaders - Student Mentorship Program

    Guiding Academic and Personal Growth for Tomorrow's Leaders

    Our organization is dedicated to empowering the next generation of leaders through our comprehensive student mentorship program. Tailored to address the challenges of academic and personal growth, this program offers guidance, support, and encouragement to young individuals. Experienced professionals serve as mentors, working closely with students to foster the development of crucial skills such as leadership, communication, and critical thinking. By investing in the mentorship of students, we aim to create a nurturing environment that propels them towards success, equipping them with the tools necessary for future leadership roles.
